Our Story

Salem Real Estate Portal began in 2010 when our founder, Rajesh Kumar, recognized the need for a more transparent and client-focused approach to real estate in Salem. Having worked in the industry for over 15 years, Rajesh saw how challenging it could be for people to navigate property transactions without proper guidance.

What started as a small office with just three team members has grown into Salem's most trusted real estate resource. Over the years, we've helped thousands of families find their dream homes, assisted businesses in securing ideal commercial spaces, and guided investors toward profitable opportunities throughout Tamil Nadu.

Today, our team of 25 dedicated professionals continues to uphold the values upon which we were founded, combining deep local knowledge with innovative technology to provide an unmatched real estate experience.
